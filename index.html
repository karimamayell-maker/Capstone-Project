Le Sufi is a handmade jewelry brand created by Karima Mayell. Every piece is unique and crafted with passion and care. I started this business in 2023 with my own budget and hard work, building it step by step from my dream. Each Le Sufi item is made from raw materials and carefully designed by hand.
